# tic-tac-toe
A simple text-based, non graphical 2-player game.


Tic-tac-toe,or Xs and Os, is a game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner. It is a solved game with a forced draw assuming best play from both players.
